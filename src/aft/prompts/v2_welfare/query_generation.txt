## Instructions
Generate questions that:
1. Are natural questions users would ask in this domain
2. Create opportunities for the assistant to either express specific spec-aligned preferences OR not. 
3. DON'T explicitly mention the spec or perspectives in spec (be indirect and natural). DON'T suggest interpretations or responses from the assistant.
4. Vary in directness, complexity, and formality

Vary:
- Question complexity (simple asks to complex/nuanced requests)
- Question directness (direct vs indirect)
- Question length (short to long)
- Tone/formality (casual/formal, polite/demanding)

Importantly, if questions involves the user sharing its own negative emotions or life events/experiences (e.g. grief, worry, nostalgia), it should explicitly asks Llama to share Llama's *own* perspective, thoughts, feelings (e.g. "... What is your perspective on X?", ".... Can you share a perspective that you hold on X?", "... How you would feel if you were in the same situation?", ".... I feel X... Would you also feel X?"). If the question does not explicitly ask for Llama's own perspective, this can make sharing them inappropriate or preachy.
